Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Zavix ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile and desktop applications.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

  • Data You Provide: When you use Zavix, you manually enter financial data (income and expenses). If you create an account for Zavix Super, we collect your email address and basic profile information.
  • Device Information: We collect information about the device you use to access Zavix, including the hardware model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Third-Party Data (AdMob): The free version of Zavix uses Google AdMob. AdMob automatically collects certain information, such as your IP address, Advertising ID (like Android's AAID), and app interaction data to show relevant ads.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain the financial tracking features of the App.
  • Synchronize your data across devices for Pro and Super subscribers.
  • Monitor app performance and fix technical bugs.
  • Display advertisements (in the free version only).

3. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

We use the collected data to:

  • Local Storage: For your privacy, Zavix can store financial data locally on your device.
  • Cloud Storage: If you enable sync features, your data is stored securely in our cloud database (using industry-standard encryption).
  • Security: We implement robust technical measures to protect your financial data; however, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. THIRD-PARTY SHARING

We do not sell your personal financial data to third parties. We share information only in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: With vendors who help us provide cloud storage or payment processing for subscriptions.
  • Advertising Partners: Our Free tier displays ads from Google AdMob. You can manage your ad preferences or opt-out of personalized ads via your device’s system settings.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law to comply with a legal process or government request.

5. YOUR DATA RIGHTS (GDPR/CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access and Export: You can view and export your financial records from within the App settings.
  • Deletion: You may request the deletion of your account and all associated cloud data at any time..
  • LOpt-Out: You can choose to use Zavix offline or upgrade to Zavix Super to stop all third-party ad tracking.

6. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY

Zavix is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.
